# Day 10 — Phishing Basics

## What is phishing?
Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers send fake messages
to trick people into sharing passwords, bank details, or clicking bad links.

## Common phishing types
- Email phishing
- SMS phishing (smishing)
- Voice phishing (vishing)
- Fake websites
- Social media scams

## Common signs of phishing
- Urgent message tone
- Unknown sender
- Spelling mistakes
- Strange links
- Requests for passwords

## What happens if someone clicks?
- Account hacked
- Data stolen
- Malware installed
- Financial loss

## How security analysts handle phishing
- Analyze email headers
- Check links and attachments
- Block sender
- Warn users
- Update security rules

## How to stay safe
- Do not click unknown links
- Check sender carefully
- Report suspicious emails
- Use MFA
